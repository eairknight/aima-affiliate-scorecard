AIMA Affiliate Lead Quality Scorecard

Use this to rate affiliate traffic quality from 1 to 10 after an affiliate has sent at least 20 AIMA Accelerator buyers.

First quality read happens on day 3. Keep tracking day 7, day 14, and day 30 because some good buyers upgrade later.

Scoring Rules

Only score an affiliate after at least 20 approved-geo AIMA buyers. Before that, show insufficient sample.

Do not include non-approved geos in the score. They are simply not payable conversions.

Start at 0 points

Condition Points
Day-3 tech-stack rate is 25%+ +7
Day-3 tech-stack rate is 20% to 24.9% +5
Day-3 tech-stack rate is 15% to 19.9% +2
Order bump rate is 40%+ +3
Order bump rate is 30% to 39.9% +1

Cap the final score at 10.

Quick Decision Rules

  1. Only pay for approved geos: US, GB, AU, CA, DE.
  2. Wait until the affiliate has at least 20 approved-geo AIMA buyers before assigning a score.
  3. By day 3, expect roughly 1 tech-stack buyer per 4 AIMA buyers.
  4. If day-3 tech stack is low but order bump rate is strong, keep watching to day 7 or day 14.
  5. If day-3 tech stack is low and order bump rate is weak, pause the affiliate.

Example

If an affiliate sends 20 US/GB/AU buyers, the day-3 goal is about 5 tech-stack buyers. 3-4 is acceptable but worth watching. 0-2 is bad unless the order bump rate is unusually strong.
